1.18 Sky Lands

This is a datapack which allows the players to travel to a new dimension when they sleep within the game. Upon sleeping, the player enters a dimension of floating islands where villages exist, ores are plentiful, and loot/ruins exist. To return back to the overworld the player must leave the sky lands by either dying or by falling out of the sky lands. In doing so the player will fall into the overworld with relative positioning to their position inside of the sky lands. Overall it adds new loot, a new dimension, and some new mechanics to create a larger survival experience. All of this was implemented using minecraft datapacks.
